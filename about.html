ABOUT US

Launched in 2023, our technology platform connects customers, delivery partners, and IIT Kharagpur eateries, serving their multiple needs. Customers can use our platform to search and discover IIT Kharagpur eateries, read and write customer-generated reviews, view and upload photos, order food delivery, book a table, and make payments while dining-out at the eateries. We provide IIT Kharagpur eateries with industry-specific marketing tools that enable them to engage and acquire customers to grow their business while also providing a reliable and efficient last-mile delivery service. Additionally, we operate a one-stop procurement solution, Hyperpure, which supplies high-quality ingredients and kitchen products to IIT Kharagpur eateries. We also provide our delivery partners with transparent and flexible earning opportunities, ensuring a seamless experience for all parties involved.
