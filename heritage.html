The embodiment of food in the spirit of the nation is an integral part of its identity. Our Heritage is the embodiment of North Indian, Chinese, and Tandoori Items. In India, highways are dotted with loc al restaurants popularly known as 'Dhabas', providing local cuisine and serving as stops for truck drivers and families. Dhabas are traditionally characterized by casu al seating on cots (called chaarpai in Hindi) and food prepared in clay ovens (tandoor). Over time Dhaba s have come to define a culture, centered on food. Our Heritage, has come with the initiative to provide the students/families of Kharagpur the mouth-watering taste of those dhabas in an airconditioned and sophisticated environment. Our food is non-monolithic. It’s simple. It's rich. It’s robust. It's diverse. Please join our chefs and staff to explore real food prepared by real people.
